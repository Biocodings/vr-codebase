Getting HipSci Gene Expression Data from iRODS
==============================================

Take the analysis_uuid from the email alert from Chris McGee, e.g. 588d838c-342f-45c7-8f97-62a66020adeb.

>imeta qu -z archive -C analysis_uuid = 588d838c-342f-45c7-8f97-62a66020adeb

Displayed:

collection: /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12

Take the directory displayed and look inside with ils e.g.:

>ils /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12

Displayed:

/archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12:
  Plate maps_hipsci_2014-02-12.xlsx
  hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12.txt
  hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12.xlsx
  hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12_Control_Probe_Profile.txt
  hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12_Sample_Probe_Profile.txt
  hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12_annotation.txt
  hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12_quantile_Sample_Probe_Profile.txt

Use iget to retrieve the files listed e.g.:

iget /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12/Plate\ maps_hipsci_2014-02-12.xlsx
iget /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12.txt
iget /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12.xlsx
iget /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12_Control_Probe_Profile.txt
iget /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12_Sample_Probe_Profile.txt
iget /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12_annotation.txt .
iget /archive/GAPI/exp/analysis/cb/82/3c/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12/hipsci_12samples_2014-02-12_quantile_Sample_Probe_Profile.txt .

Getting HipSci Genotyping Data from iRODS
=========================================

Take the analysis_uuid from the email alert from genotyping, e.g. 588d838c-342f-45c7-8f97-62a66020adeb.

>imeta qu -z archive -C analysis_uuid = 89a2ba52-a1f2-45bc-bd47-63297f6fcde0

Displays:

collection: /archive/GAPI/gen/analysis/19/7d/27/coreex_hips

>ils /archive/GAPI/gen/analysis/19/7d/27/coreex_hips

Displays: 

/archive/GAPI/gen/analysis/19/7d/27/coreex_hips:
  C- /archive/GAPI/gen/analysis/19/7d/27/coreex_hips/20140213

So then look in the subdirectory:

>ils /archive/GAPI/gen/analysis/19/7d/27/coreex_hips/20140213

Displays:

/archive/GAPI/gen/analysis/19/7d/27/coreex_hips/20140213:
  coreex_hips_20140213.fcr.txt.gz
  genotyping.db

Now get the gz file (genotyping.db isn't required):

>iget /archive/GAPI/gen/analysis/19/7d/27/coreex_hips/20140213/coreex_hips_20140213.fcr.txt.gz
